About me

As a frontend developer, I am passionate about designing and developing interactive, responsive websites and web applications that enhance user experiences. With expertise in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, TypeScript, and frameworks like React.js, Bootstrap, and Tailwind, I focus on creating modern, performant solutions that solve real-world problems through clean code and innovative design.

In addition to my core frontend skills, I have hands-on experience from projects like building a personal portfolio, a movie & TV show browsing platform, an eCommerce bookstore, and a blood bank donation site. As a Computer Science student at Menoufia University, I stay up-to-date with emerging technologies and best practices, including responsive web design, animations, and API integrations, to ensure my work is cutting-edge and effective.

I am dedicated to problem-solving, teamwork, and continuously learning to deliver the best value in dynamic environments. Eager to contribute to exciting projects, I seek new opportunities to apply my skills and make a meaningful impact. Let's connect and explore how we can collaborate on innovative web solutions.
